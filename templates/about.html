About CyberMadad

CyberMadad is a cyber-fraud support and awareness platform designed to help individuals who have been affected by online scams, phishing attacks, fake websites, and other forms of digital fraud.

In todayâ€™s digital world, cyber crimes are increasing rapidly, while law-enforcement agencies are often overloaded with cases. CyberMadad aims to bridge this gap by providing a structured platform where victims can report incidents and receive guidance from trained cybersecurity students and researchers.

Our platform works strictly on ethical and legal principles. All investigations are conducted using OSINT (Open-Source Intelligence) techniques only. We do not perform hacking, illegal access, or intrusive activities of any kind.

Victim privacy and data security are our top priorities. Personal details are shared with investigators only when the victim gives explicit consent. CyberMadad is not a law-enforcement agency and does not replace official cyber crime reporting channels.

Our Mission

To spread cyber awareness, support cyber-fraud victims, and empower students to gain real-world cybersecurity experience in a responsible and ethical manner.

Our Vision

To create a safer digital ecosystem by connecting victims, cybersecurity learners, and awareness initiatives on a single transparent platform.
